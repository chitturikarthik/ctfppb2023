About The Conference

Plant biology has seen tremendous progress in basic and applied research in the last few years. With the advent of advanced tools and techniques, researchers could understand the morphological, physiological, biochemical, and molecular aspects of plant growth, development, and stress responses. Omics approaches have enabled gaining insights into the above aspects at different levels, and recently, the importance of integrated omics has been demonstrated in dissecting the complex traits underlying growth, development, and response to environmental cues. Despite the progress made, challenges do persist in pursuing advanced research in plant biology. Given this, an international conference on Current Trends and Future Prospects of Plant Biology has been organized by the silver jubilee Department of Plant Sciences, School of Life Sciences, University of Hyderabad, Telangana, India, during February 23-25, 2023. The objective of this conference is to bring together the global research community to:

  • Discuss new concepts, tools, and techniques in plant biology.
  • Enable researchers to present their research progress and challenges to be addressed.
  • Identify new directions in collaborative research at national and international levels and
  • Recognize the young scientists working in frontier areas of plant biology who made significant contributions.




TARGET PARTICIPANTS


Students, Researchers, Faculties, Scientists, Entrepreneurs, and Industry Professionals from India and abroad working in any discipline of plant sciences.



MAJOR HIGHLIGHTS OF THE EVENT


  • First largest meeting in India to bring together the plant science research community post-pandemic.
  • Plenary talks by eminent speakers working in different aspects of omics in plant biology.
  • Keynote lectures by distinguished researchers that represent the diversity of fields of expertise.
  • Oral and poster presentations by young scientists to showcase their research progress.
  • The conference also provides a platform to plant science-based industries and start-ups to meet and discuss possible collaborations.

Department of Plant Sciences at a Glance

The Department of Plant Sciences (est. 1993) is renowned for imparting high- quality teaching and research in the frontier areas of plant sciences and microbiology. The vision of the Department is to discover and exploit plant and microbiological resources for the betterment of the environment and human welfare through systematic and focused research, and teaching in plant and microbiological sciences. The teaching and research in the Department are supported by UGC-SAP (DRS-1, Phase 1), DST-FIST Level I and II (Phase 1 and 2), and the Institute of Eminence grant awarded to the University of Hyderabad. Further, the faculties of the Department have secured extra-mural grants from national and international funding bodies to pursue individual and collaborative research. The outcomes of the research projects are published in peer-reviewed journals of international repute, and the faculties have also filed several national and international patents. The Department also has the distinction of establishing the state-of-art facility 'Repository of Tomato' Genomics Resources,' which is a DBT-Center of Excellence.
